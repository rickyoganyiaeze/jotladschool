Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to enroll your child at Jotlad Group of Schools.

1

Obtain Application Form

Purchase admission form from the school office or download from our website. Forms are available for both Nursery/Basic and college sections.

2

Complete & Submit Form

Fill the application form completely with accurate information. Submit along with required documents including passport photographs, birth certificate, and previous school records.

3

Entrance Assessment

Students seeking admission into Basic and college sections will undergo an entrance assessment to determine appropriate class placement.

4

Interview

Successful candidates and their parents/guardians will be invited for an interview with the school management.

5

Admission Offer

Successful candidates will receive an admission offer letter with details of school fees, uniform requirements, and resumption date.

6

Acceptance & Registration

Accept the admission offer by paying the required fees and completing the registration process before the deadline.

Admission Requirements

Requirements for each school section are listed below.

Nursery & Basic School

Creche, Nursery & Basic 1-6

Required Documents:

  • Completed application form
  • 4 recent passport photographs
  • Birth certificate (photocopy)
  • Immunization record
  • Previous school report (if applicable)
  • Transfer certificate (if applicable)

Age Requirements:

  • Creche: 3 months - 2 years
  • Playgroup: 2 - 3 years
  • Nursery: 3 - 5 years
  • Basic 1: 5 years by September
Request Form

Model College (college)

JSS 1 - SS 3

Required Documents:

  • Completed application form
  • 4 recent passport photographs
  • Birth certificate (photocopy)
  • Basic school leaving certificate
  • Basic school testimonial/result
  • Transfer certificate (for mid-term admission)

Entry Requirements:

  • JSS 1: Basic 6 certificate
  • JSS 2-3: Transfer exam required
  • SS 1: BECE certificate
  • SS 2-3: Transfer exam required
