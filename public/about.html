Our Story

Jotlad Group of Schools was established in 1995 with a vision to provide quality education that nurtures both academic excellence and moral character. What started as a small nursery school has grown into a comprehensive educational institution comprising Jotlad Nursery & Basic School and Jotlad Model College.

For nearly three decades, we have been committed to developing well-rounded individuals who are not only academically sound but also morally upright and socially responsible. Our approach to education combines traditional values with modern teaching methodologies to prepare students for the challenges of the 21st century.

Our school community is built on the foundation of mutual respect, discipline, and a shared commitment to excellence. We take pride in our dedicated teachers, supportive parents, and most importantly, our outstanding students who continue to make us proud through their achievements.

Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our commitment to educational excellence.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, quality education that develops the intellectual, moral, physical, and social capacities of every child, preparing them to become responsible citizens and future leaders who can compete favorably in a global society.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for academic excellence, moral integrity, and the holistic development of students, producing graduates who are equipped with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for success in life.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Jotlad Group of Schools.

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in academics, character, and all aspects of school life.

Integrity

Upholding honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in all our dealings.

Discipline

Instilling self-discipline, respect for rules, and responsibility in our students.

Innovation

Embracing modern teaching methods and technology to enhance learning outcomes.

Teamwork

Collaborating with parents, teachers, and the community for student success.

Godliness

Nurturing spiritual growth and moral values based on reverence for God.
